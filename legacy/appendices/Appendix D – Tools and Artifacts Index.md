# Appendix D – Tools and Artifacts Index

This appendix provides a comprehensive quick-reference index of the project management tools, techniques, and artifacts covered throughout the PMP 2026 Study Guide. It maps these items to the specific chapters where they are discussed in context and indicates their applicability across different delivery approaches.

> Note: Chapter-reference links in this appendix are being updated to match this repository’s chapter structure. For v1, broken chapter links have been removed; references remain as text.


**Context Labels:**
- **All**: Applicable to predictive, agile, and hybrid approaches
- **Predictive**: Primarily used in traditional/waterfall projects
- **Agile**: Primarily used in agile/iterative projects  
- **Hybrid**: Used in hybrid/mixed approach projects

## Cross-References and Alternative Names

Some tools and techniques are known by multiple names or have closely related variants:

- **Fishbone Diagram** = **Ishikawa Diagram** = **Cause-and-Effect Diagram**
- **Burn-down Chart** = **Sprint Burndown Chart**
- **Burn-up Chart** = **Release Burnup Chart**
- **Float** = **Slack** (in schedule analysis)
- **Activity-on-Node** = **Precedence Diagramming Method**
- **Backlog Grooming** = **Backlog Refinement**
- **User Story** = **Story** (in agile contexts)
- **Definition of Done** = **DoD**
- **Work Breakdown Structure** = **WBS**
- **Responsibility Assignment Matrix** = **RACI Matrix** = **RAM**
- **Power/Interest Grid** = **Power/Interest Matrix**
- **Stakeholder Engagement Matrix** = **Stakeholder Engagement Assessment Matrix**
