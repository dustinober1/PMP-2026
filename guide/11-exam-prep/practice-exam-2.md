## Questions

### Question 1

A project has a BAC of $400,000. At the midpoint, the team has completed work worth $180,000 (EV) but has spent $220,000 (AC). What is the CPI?

A) 0.82  
B) 0.90  
C) 1.10  
D) 1.22  

---

### Question 2

A servant leader would MOST likely:

A) Make all decisions for the team to ensure efficiency  
B) Remove impediments and support the team's success  
C) Direct team members on exactly how to complete their tasks  
D) Prioritize schedule adherence over team well-being  

---

### Question 3

During a project, the sponsor suddenly announces that the strategic priority has changed and the project benefits are now questionable. What should the PM do?

A) Continue the project as planned since it was approved  
B) Recommend reassessing the business case and project viability  
C) Ignore the announcement and focus on deliverables  
D) Cancel the project immediately without further analysis  

---

### Question 4

Which conflict resolution technique results in a win-win outcome where both parties' concerns are fully addressed?

A) Forcing  
B) Compromising  
C) Collaborating  
D) Avoiding  

---

### Question 5

A project's critical path has a duration of 45 days. The team identifies an opportunity to complete a non-critical activity (with 5 days of float) 3 days early. What impact does this have on the project duration?

A) Project duration is reduced by 3 days  
B) Project duration is reduced by 5 days  
C) No impact on project duration  
D) Project duration increases by 3 days  

---

### Question 6

During planning, the team discovers that a key assumption in the project charter is incorrect. What should the PM do?

A) Proceed with planning and address it later  
B) Update the assumptions log and assess impact on project plans  
C) Cancel the project due to invalid assumptions  
D) Ignore it since the charter is already approved  

---

### Question 7

A team member consistently delivers high-quality work but struggles with communication. According to situational leadership, the PM should:

A) Treat all team members the same regardless of skill  
B) Provide focused coaching on communication skills  
C) Replace the team member with someone more skilled  
D) Remove communication responsibilities entirely  

---

### Question 8

In agile, what is the primary purpose of a sprint retrospective?

A) To demonstrate completed work to stakeholders  
B) To plan the next sprint's work  
C) To reflect on the process and identify improvements  
D) To groom the product backlog  

---

### Question 9

Which document formally authorizes the project manager to apply resources to project activities?

A) Project management plan  
B) Scope statement  
C) Project charter  
D) Work breakdown structure  

---

### Question 10

A project manager is using the Power/Interest Grid to analyze stakeholders. A stakeholder with high power and low interest should be:

A) Managed closely  
B) Kept satisfied  
C) Kept informed  
D) Monitored
