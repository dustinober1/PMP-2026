## Quick Study Tips

1. **Read the question first** ‚Äì Know what you're looking for before reading the scenario
2. **Eliminate wrong answers** ‚Äì Three wrong, one best
3. **Think PMI** ‚Äì Their philosophy, not your organization's practice
4. **Manage time** ‚Äì Don't spend too long on any one question
5. **Trust your preparation** ‚Äì You've done the work
