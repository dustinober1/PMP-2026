# Chapter 2: Strategic Alignment and Business Environment

Welcome to Chapter 2 of the PMP Exam Prep 2026 study guide. This chapter focuses on ensuring projects align with organizational strategy and deliver sustainable business value.

## Overview

This chapter covers critical aspects of how projects connect to the broader business environment, including:

- **Strategy and Portfolio Alignment** - Understanding how projects fit into organizational strategy
- **Benefits, Value, and Sustainability** - Managing and realizing business value from project outcomes
- **Compliance, Risk, and Governance** - Navigating regulatory requirements and governance structures
- **Organizational Change and Readiness** - Managing the human side of project implementation
- **Program Management** - Coordinating multiple projects to achieve strategic benefits

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will be able to:

- Explain how projects align with organizational strategy and portfolio management
- Define and manage benefits realization throughout the project lifecycle
- Navigate compliance requirements and governance frameworks
- Apply change management principles to ensure project success
- Understand program management and its relationship to projects

## Why This Matters for the PMP Exam

The Business Environment domain now represents **26%** of the PMP exam (up from 8% in previous versions). This reflects PMI's emphasis on:

- Strategic thinking in project management
- Value delivery beyond just completing scope
- Understanding the organizational context
- Ethical and sustainable practices
