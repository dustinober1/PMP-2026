## ðŸ’¡ Practical Application
Strategic alignment isn't a one-time activity during project initiation. It's an ongoing discipline that requires:

- **Continuous validation** that projects remain strategically relevant as priorities evolve.
- **Portfolio-level governance** that balances competing initiatives and resource constraints.
- **Adaptive roadmapping** that responds to market changes while maintaining strategic direction.

::: tip Sarah's Story: The Strategic Shift
Six months into her expense system project, Sarah's organization acquired a competitor with a similar system. Suddenly, her "strategic alignment" changed from building a new system to integrating with an existing one. This real-world pivot is exactly what the 2026 PMP exam expects you to navigate.
:::
