# Practice Exam 1

Test your readiness for the PMP certification with this practice exam.

::: info Exam Simulation
This practice exam simulates the real PMP exam experience. Set a timer for 80 minutes and answer all questions without looking at the answers.
:::

## Instructions

- **Questions**: 50 scenario-based questions
- **Time Limit**: 80 minutes (same pace as real exam)
- **Domains**: People (33%), Process (41%), Business (26%)
- **Answer key**: Located at the end of this page

---

## Questions

### Question 1

A project manager notices that a key stakeholder has stopped attending status meetings and is unresponsive to emails. The project is entering a critical phase. What should the PM do FIRST?

A) Continue with the project and document the stakeholder's absence  
B) Escalate to the sponsor immediately  
C) Reach out to the stakeholder directly to understand their concerns  
D) Remove the stakeholder from the communication plan  

---

### Question 2

A project is 60% complete with SPI = 0.85 and CPI = 0.92. What does this indicate?

A) Ahead of schedule and under budget  
B) Behind schedule and over budget  
C) Behind schedule and under budget  
D) Ahead of schedule and over budget  

---

### Question 3

During a sprint retrospective, the team identifies that unclear user stories are causing rework. What should the Scrum Master recommend?

A) Skip retrospectives until the project stabilizes  
B) Improve the Definition of Ready for user stories  
C) Assign blame to the Product Owner  
D) Extend sprint duration to allow more time  

---

### Question 4

A project manager receives a change request that would significantly impact the project scope and timeline. What should they do FIRST?

A) Implement the change immediately to satisfy the stakeholder  
B) Reject the change to protect the baseline  
C) Perform impact analysis on scope, schedule, cost, and quality  
D) Convene the Change Control Board immediately  

---

### Question 5

A team member approaches the PM with concerns about unethical behavior by another team member. What should the PM do FIRST?

A) Ignore the concern unless there is documented evidence  
B) Tell the team member to handle it themselves  
C) Listen carefully and gather more information  
D) Immediately terminate the accused team member  

---

### Question 6

Which project selection method uses the formula PV = FV / (1 + r)^n?

A) Payback Period  
B) Internal Rate of Return  
C) Net Present Value  
D) Benefit-Cost Ratio  

---

### Question 7

A project manager is leading a distributed team across five time zones. What is the MOST important action to ensure effective collaboration?

A) Require all team members to be online during the PM's business hours  
B) Establish clear communication protocols and overlapping work hours  
C) Use email exclusively to avoid synchronization issues  
D) Meet only when absolutely necessary to minimize inconvenience  

---

### Question 8

During project execution, a risk that was identified in the risk register occurs. The team had prepared a response plan. What should the PM do?

A) Document it as a lesson learned and move on  
B) Implement the planned risk response  
C) Escalate to the sponsor for guidance  
D) Update the risk register and wait for further developments  

---

### Question 9

A stakeholder requests a new feature near the end of the project. The project manager analyzes the request and determines it would add significant value but delay the timeline by two weeks. What should the PM recommend?

A) Reject the request because the project is nearly complete  
B) Present the trade-off analysis to the sponsor for a decision  
C) Implement the change without approval to satisfy the stakeholder  
D) Add the feature to a future project phase without analysis  

---

### Question 10

In the Tuckman model, which stage is characterized by conflict and disagreement as team members assert themselves?

A) Forming  
B) Storming  
C) Norming  
D) Performing

## Answer Key

| Question | Answer | Explanation                                                                          |
| -------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1        | C      | Always reach out directly first to understand stakeholder concerns before escalating |
| 2        | B      | SPI < 1 = behind schedule; CPI < 1 = over budget                                     |
| 3        | B      | Improving Definition of Ready addresses root cause of unclear stories                |
| 4        | C      | Always perform impact analysis before approving or rejecting changes                 |
| 5        | C      | Listen and gather information before taking action                                   |
| 6        | C      | NPV uses the present value formula                                                   |
| 7        | B      | Clear protocols and overlapping hours enable distributed team success                |
| 8        | B      | Implement the planned response; that's why you planned it                            |
| 9        | B      | Present trade-offs and let the sponsor (who has authority) decide                    |
| 10       | B      | Storming is characterized by conflict as team members assert themselves              |
