## Additional Study Topics

For complete mastery of Chapter 3, ensure you understand:

1. **Building Teams (3.1)**
   - The five stages of Tuckman's Model.
   - Creating Psychological Safety.
   - Leading Virtual and Global teams.

2. **Servant Leadership (3.2)**
   - The shift from command-and-control to empowerment.
   - Situational Leadership (S1-S4).
   - Emotional Intelligence quadrants.

3. **Conflict Management (3.3)**
   - The 5 Thomas-Kilmann conflict modes.
   - When to escalate vs. when to facilitate.

4. **Motivation (3.4)**
   - Herzberg, Maslow, and McClelland's theories.
   - Theory X vs. Theory Y assumptions.
