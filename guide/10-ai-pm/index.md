# Chapter 10: AI and Project Management

Welcome to Chapter 10 of the PMP Exam Prep 2026 study guide. This chapter covers how artificial intelligence is transforming project management and how to use AI effectively and ethically in your projects.

::: info 2026 Exam Focus
The 2026 PMP exam recognizes AI as an emerging trend that project managers must understand. This chapter prepares you to answer AI-related scenario questions and apply AI responsibly in practice.
:::

## Why This Chapter Matters

AI is transforming project management:

- **Automation** of routine tasks frees PMs for strategic work
- **Predictive analytics** enable proactive risk management
- **Enhanced decision-making** through data-driven insights
- **Improved communication** via AI-assisted drafting and translation
- **New challenges** around ethics, privacy, and governance

Understanding AI is no longer optional‚Äîit's essential for modern project managers.
