# 9.2 Project Closure

Closure is not just the end of work; it is the **Transfer of Value**. In the 2026 PMP exam, your job as a Project Manager ends only when the operational owner is fully empowered to succeed and the team's wisdom has been harvested for the enterprise.

---

## üèÅ The Final Protocol
A project must be closed formally, even if it was terminated early or canceled due to strategic shifts.

<div class="closure-grid">
  <div class="closure-card">
    <div class="closure-title">1. Formal Acceptance</div>
    <div class="closure-tag">The Goal</div>
    <p>Obtain written sign-off from the Sponsor or Customer. Ensure all <strong>Acceptance Criteria</strong> have been met.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="closure-card">
    <div class="closure-title">2. Operational Handoff</div>
    <div class="closure-tag">The Transition</div>
    <p>Deliver the training, documentation, and "Runbooks" to the people who will live with the product daily.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="closure-card">
    <div class="closure-title">3. Admin & Contract</div>
    <div class="closure-tag">The Cleanup</div>
    <p>Close out all vendor contracts, settle final payments, and archive the project records in the OPA library.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="closure-card">
    <div class="closure-title">4. Team Release</div>
    <div class="closure-tag">The People</div>
    <p>Formally release the team members to their next units. <strong>Celebrate the success</strong> to boost morale for future projects.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## üß† Harvesting Lessons
The **Lessons Learned Register** is the most valuable output of closure. It should not be a "blame session" but an objective look at:
- **What went right**: How can we repeat this success?
- **What went wrong**: How can the next PM avoid this trap?
- **Innovations**: What new tools or tricks did we discover?

::: tip üí° 2026 Shift: Canceled Projects
If a project is canceled mid-flight, do not just walk away. Your FIRST duty is to <strong>Perform Administrative Closure</strong>‚Äîdocumenting why it was canceled and archiving the partial deliverables and lessons.
:::

---

## üîÑ Methodology Nuances
- **Predictive**: Closure occurs at the end of each phase and at the final project conclusion.
- **Agile**: "Closure" is a continuous pulse. Retrospectives provide closure at every sprint, while the final closure occurs when the Product Owner declares the backlog "Satisfied" or funding expires.
