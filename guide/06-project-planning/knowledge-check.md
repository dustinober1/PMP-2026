## Additional Study Topics
For full Chapter 6 proficiency, ensure you can:
1.  **Calculate Float**: Determining which tasks are "Critical."
2.  **Estimate PERT**: Using the Beta distribution for uncertainty.
3.  **Evaluate Leveling**: Understanding the trade-off between resource limits and schedule dates.
4.  **Audit the WBS**: Ensuring the 100% rule is followed.
