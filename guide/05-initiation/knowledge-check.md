## Additional Study Topics

For complete mastery of Chapter 5, ensure you understand:

1. **Project Selection (5.1)**
   - NPV, IRR, and BCR (Benefit-Cost Ratio).
   - Opportunity Cost vs. Sunk Cost.

2. **The Project Charter (5.2)**
   - High-level scope, budget, and milestones.
   - The role of the Project Sponsor.

3. **Constraints and Assumptions (5.3)**
   - Maintaining the Assumptions Log.
   - The Triple Constraint relationship.

4. **Kick-off & Strategy (5.4)**
   - Selecting the right delivery approach (Predictive, Agile, Hybrid).
   - The difference between an Initiation Kick-off and a Planning Kick-off.
