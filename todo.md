# Plan for Incorporating 5 Full-Length Exams into the Study Guide

## Overview
This document outlines the plan for incorporating 5 full-length exams into the PMP study guide. The exams will be designed to simulate the actual PMP exam experience and cover all domains and topics as specified in the 2026 PMP Exam Content Outline (ECO).

## Structure and Format

### Exam Structure
- **Number of Questions**: 185 questions per exam (175 scored, 10 unscored pretest questions)
- **Time Limit**: 240 minutes (4 hours)
- **Question Types**:
  - Multiple Choice (Single Response)
  - Multiple Response (Select All That Apply)
  - Drag-and-Drop (Sequencing, Matching)
  - Point-and-Click (Hotspot on diagrams/charts)
  - Scenario-Based Questions (Case Studies)

### Content Requirements
- **Domains Covered**:
  - **People (33%)**: Managing conflict, leading teams, stakeholder engagement, communication, and knowledge transfer.
  - **Process (41%)**: Project integration, scope, schedule, cost, quality, resource, communication, risk, procurement, and stakeholder management.
  - **Business Environment (26%)**: Project governance, compliance, organizational change, and external business environment.

- **Methodology Distribution**:
  - Predictive: 40%
  - Agile: 30%
  - Hybrid: 30%

## Organization and Naming Conventions

### Exam Naming Convention
- **Format**: `practice-exam-{number}.md`
- **Location**: `guide/11-exam-prep/`

### Example Naming
1. `practice-exam-1.md`
2. `practice-exam-2.md`
3. `practice-exam-3.md`
4. `practice-exam-4.md`
5. `practice-exam-5.md`

### Exam Focus Areas
1. **Practice Exam 1**: Fundamentals (Core People, Process, and Business Environment domains)
2. **Practice Exam 2**: Agility (Scrum, Hybrid models, and adaptive servant leadership)
3. **Practice Exam 3**: Integration (High-complexity scenarios combining risk, quality, and stakeholder conflict)
4. **Practice Exam 4**: Advanced Topics (AI in project management, sustainability, and compliance)
5. **Practice Exam 5**: Comprehensive (Full simulation of the PMP exam with all domains and methodologies)

## Topics and Domains Coverage

### Exam 1: Fundamentals
- **People**: Team development, conflict management, stakeholder engagement
- **Process**: Project integration, scope, schedule, cost, quality
- **Business Environment**: Governance, compliance, organizational change

### Exam 2: Agility
- **People**: Servant leadership, team empowerment, virtual collaboration
- **Process**: Agile methodologies, Scrum, Kanban, adaptive planning
- **Business Environment**: Agile governance, compliance in adaptive environments

### Exam 3: Integration
- **People**: Stakeholder conflict resolution, communication strategies
- **Process**: Risk management, quality control, procurement
- **Business Environment**: Strategic alignment, business value realization

### Exam 4: Advanced Topics
- **People**: Knowledge transfer, coaching, and mentoring
- **Process**: AI tools for estimation, risk detection, and reporting
- **Business Environment**: Sustainability (ESG), external environment analysis

### Exam 5: Comprehensive
- **All Domains**: Full coverage of People, Process, and Business Environment
- **All Methodologies**: Predictive, Agile, and Hybrid

## Timeline and Milestones

### Phase 1: Planning and Research (Week 1-2)
- **Task**: Finalize exam structure, content requirements, and naming conventions
- **Milestone**: Approval of the plan and structure

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Week 3-8)
- **Task**: Develop questions for each exam based on the identified topics and domains
- **Milestone**: Completion of all 5 exams with 185 questions each

### Phase 3: Review and Validation (Week 9-10)
- **Task**: Review questions for accuracy, relevance, and alignment with PMP exam standards
- **Milestone**: Validation of all questions and exams

### Phase 4: Integration (Week 11-12)
- **Task**: Integrate the exams into the study guide and ensure they are accessible and functional
- **Milestone**: Successful integration and testing of all exams

### Phase 5: Final Review and Launch (Week 13)
- **Task**: Conduct a final review and launch the exams
- **Milestone**: Official launch of the 5 full-length exams

## Implementation Steps

1. **Create Exam Files**: Set up the markdown files for each exam in the `guide/11-exam-prep/` directory.
2. **Develop Questions**: Write questions for each exam, ensuring they cover the specified domains and methodologies.
3. **Review and Validate**: Review the questions for accuracy and alignment with the PMP exam standards.
4. **Integrate Exams**: Add the exams to the study guide and ensure they are properly linked and accessible.
5. **Test and Launch**: Conduct thorough testing to ensure the exams function as intended and launch them officially.

## Conclusion
This plan provides a structured approach to incorporating 5 full-length exams into the PMP study guide. By following this plan, we ensure that the exams are comprehensive, aligned with the PMP exam standards, and effectively integrated into the study guide.
