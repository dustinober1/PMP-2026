## Instructions

Use this template to calculate your performance on practice exams and track your progress over time. The scoring system mirrors the actual PMP exam proficiency levels.

## Proficiency Levels

The PMP exam reports performance in four levels for each domain:

- **Above Target**: Exceeds minimum requirements significantly
- **Target**: Meets minimum requirements 
- **Below Target**: Approaches but doesn't meet minimum requirements
- **Needs Improvement**: Well below minimum requirements

## Scoring Guidelines

### Overall Score Targets
- **Above Target**: 80%+ (148+ correct out of 185)
- **Target**: 70-79% (130-147 correct out of 185)
- **Below Target**: 60-69% (111-129 correct out of 185)
- **Needs Improvement**: <60% (<111 correct out of 185)

### Domain Score Targets
- **Above Target**: 75%+ correct in domain
- **Target**: 60-74% correct in domain
- **Below Target**: 50-59% correct in domain
- **Needs Improvement**: <50% correct in domain

## Study Resource Recommendations

### Based on Performance Level

**Above Target Overall**:
- Focus on maintaining performance
- Light review with flashcards
- Day-before-exam summary
- One final practice exam

**Target Overall**:
- Review weak domain chapters
- Additional practice questions
- Use quick reference guide
- Focus on scenario-based questions

**Below Target Overall**:
- Intensive chapter review for weak domains
- Multiple additional practice exams
- Use all study aids (flashcards, concept map, quick reference)
- Consider study group or additional training

**Needs Improvement Overall**:
- Comprehensive content review
- Start with fundamental concepts
- Consider formal training course
- Extended study timeline recommended

### Domain-Specific Resources

**People Domain Improvement**:
- Chapters 6-9 (Leadership, Teams, Communication, Conflict)
- Focus on servant leadership and situational leadership
- Practice stakeholder management scenarios
- Review Tuckman's team development model

**Process Domain Improvement**:
- Chapters 10-14, 23 (Planning, Execution, Monitoring, Closing)
- Master earned value management formulas
- Practice schedule and cost calculations
- Review agile practices and frameworks

**Business Environment Improvement**:
- Chapters 15-18, 24 (Strategy, Benefits, Compliance, Change)
- Focus on benefits realization and value delivery
- Review organizational change management
- Study compliance and governance frameworks

## Exam Day Preparation Checklist

When you're consistently scoring at Target level or above:

**One Week Before**:
- [ ] Final practice exam completed
- [ ] All weak areas reviewed
- [ ] Day-before summary reviewed
- [ ] Exam logistics confirmed (location, time, ID requirements)

**Day Before Exam**:
- [ ] Light review only - no intensive studying
- [ ] Review day-before summary from quick reference guide
- [ ] Get good night's sleep
- [ ] Prepare exam day materials

**Exam Day**:
- [ ] Arrive early and relaxed
- [ ] Bring required identification
- [ ] Stay calm and confident
- [ ] Use time management strategies practiced
- [ ] Trust your preparation and instincts

---

*Remember: The goal is not perfection, but consistent performance at the Target level or above. Focus your study efforts on areas showing Needs Improvement or Below Target performance.*
